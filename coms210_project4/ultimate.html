Atletico Madrid

Summary

Club Atlético de Madrid, better known as Atlético de Madrid, is a Spanish football club in the city of Madrid, founded on April 26, 1903, being one of the longest active soccer clubs in Spain. Since the 2017-18 season, they have played their home games at the Wanda Metropolitano stadium, with a capacity for 68,456 spectators. Identified by its red and white colors - of which it receives the nickname «rojiblancos» or «colchoneros» - it is one of the most successful Spanish and Europeans clubs. On a social level, it has more than 130,000 members, of which more than 58,000 are subscribers and 850 clubs, 150 of them outside Spain. In addition, it has more than 40 million followers on social networks in 2020 and is the third football club with the most supporters in Spain. Economically, the team occupies thirteenth place in the list of income of European clubs, with an amount of 367.6 million euros per season, and an estimated value of 1.2 billion euros.
